Resort Rio Goa celebrates the authentic flavours of Gujarati, Rajasthani and Jain cuisine, taking cultural norms into consideration with a separate kitchen and low yet comfortable seats for an absolutely traditional experience.
The award-winning restaurant is known across Goa for its consistency and quality, boasting the best thalis in the region.

Experiences

Experience a blend of luxury and comfort at Aur Jalsa restaurant, nestled in Resort Rio. Indulge in exquisite culinary delights, meticulously prepared by our talented chefs, while enjoying breathtaking views of the surrounding beauty. Immerse yourself in a world of culinary excellence and unforgettable dining experiences.

Pure
Vegetarian Thali

Enjoy the flavours of a delicious Pure Vegetarian Thali at the restaurant Aur Jalsa.
A selection of delectable vegetarian meals are featured in our thoughtfully crafted thali, which is made from only the best ingredients.

Lower
Seating

At the restaurant Aur Jalsa, enjoy the appeal of low seating. You can unwind and relax while enjoying your lunch thanks to our warm and inviting floor sitting arrangement. Enjoy a traditional dining experience where low tables and comfortable chairs create a cosy ambiance.
