We take the stress out of organising destination weddings in Goa with a complete suite of services that can be customised according to your vision. Imagine aromatic floral mandaps set on verdant green lawns, themed sangeet nights with colour and verve, lavish receptions that soak in the colours of Goa's natural beauty. Or even simple, rustic furniture overseeing vows at sunset for small intimate weddings, tea parties for an unconventional late afternoon do or pheras at sunrise with family looking on.

Go big with our hospitality special featuring two separate resorts in a single location - Resort Rio and Rio Royale - one exclusively for the bride and the other for the groom, so you can literally come together at your destination wedding in Goa. Resort Rio Goa celebrates you by offering the largest banquet hall in north Goa, 10 acres of gorgeous riverside locales, and endless flexible indoor and outdoor venues that can cater to a range of events, including a sound-proof club that promises an unending night of stress-free revelry.
